Little remind of the rules :

The users choose an option of a token of the pool, and the one who achieves the best performance on the given time allows those who have chosen it to win the total amount of money involved. The starting value is the one at the moment of the launch of the timer, the value retained at the end is the one at the end of the given time (12h / 24h). The sharing of the winnings between the winners is done in proportion to the sums of each. Users who have subscribed to the losing option lose their stake.
