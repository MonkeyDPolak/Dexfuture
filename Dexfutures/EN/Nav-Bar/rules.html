Dexfutures
Disclaimer:
DexFutures is a platform to connect its users around a common interest, the Cryptocurrency and its financial future. In no case DexFutures promises any remuneration or gain to its users. As with any operation in the field of crypto-currencies, informed users already know that only money that can be lost should be used. Users also agree to comply with the legislation in force according to their personal situation before using the platform.



Users have access to several pools, composed of 2 tokens.
These pools give the initial value of each of the tokens at a given time, being noted that the starting value is defined by the chronometer.
These values are given every day at fixed hours (xxh and xxh), and determine the limit of the option taking for the user, and the payment period for the winners.
The user chooses one of the tokens, and takes an option on it: it is the one of the two tokens that will have the better (or less bad) performance that will be the winning option.
The option contract contains the following formula: If the option wins, then the bets are returned to the users who subscribed to that option and the total bets of the losing options are paid to them.
Winning options win the total amount of the losing options bets.
The distribution is made between each of the winners in proportion to the options taken on the sum to be redistributed.



Once the options are taken, the user's tokens are blocked until the end of the limited time (12 or 24 hours).
