DexFutures is a decentralized exchange that allows users to place options on token peers, over a limited period of time, based on an initial value and performance.
WhitePaper RoadMap
WhitePaper


DexFutures allows its users to participate in option taking under the only condition of holding a WEB3 compatible portfolio.


DexFutures allows to take an option on the performance of a token in a pool over a given time.
The winner option allows the user who has subscribed to it to win the sums of the users who have chosen the opposite option.


Pools reflect the value of two tokens, whose value is defined on the basis of a limited time and whose option lasts until the end of it.


The options taken by users are based on a fixed timer.
Users have until the end of the timer to take their option.
The options taken by all parties have a limit in the value to be retained (reference position), and in the final value. At the end of the timer, the crypto-currency with the best performance wins the game.
Note: in case of a global drop of the pair, the crypto-currency with the lowest performance wins the game.


Users should expect a 5% fee per option.
The fees inherent to the use of the blockchain are independent from Dexfutures.


The DFX token is based on the BSC CHAIN.
This token is proprietary to the site, and will allow holdeurs to benefit from advantages in the future.
